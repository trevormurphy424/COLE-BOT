**Pins, as defined in RX_Code.ino:**

*Motors correspond to their respective side of the h-bridge, where left is the low-numbered pins and the vehicle is viewed from the rear.*

- Left Drive Motor:
    - H-Bridge Pin 1 (ENA): Arduino 6
    - H-Bridge Pin 2 (IN1): Arduino 5
    - H-Bridge Pin 7 (IN2): Arduino 7
- Right Drive Motor:
    - H-Bridge Pin 9 (ENB): Arduino 3
    - H-Bridge Pin 10 (IN3): Arduino 2
    - H-Bridge Pin 15 (IN4): Arduino 4
- Claw Arm Servo:
    - PWM: Arduino A1
- Claw Servo:
    - PWM: Arduino A0
